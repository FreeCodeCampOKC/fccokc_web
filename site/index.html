Join Us

freeCodeCamp Oklahoma is a group for those who want to learn to code throughout the Oklahoma state area.

Meetings

We meet up every Sunday afternoon in person at StarSpace46 and on Twitch, in order to supplement the online learning with in-person guidance and instruction. We do our best to keep you up-to-date and moving forward in your career as web/software developers!

Chat

We also have a Slack room where we can have real-time discussions and offer help and advice when needed. Not sure how to use Slack? Check out our guide on getting started with Slack for more info.

Code of Conduct

Our Code of Conduct & Anti-Harassment Policy details the expectations that we have for all contributors. We value your participation and hope that by communicating these expectations widely we can all enjoy a harassment-free environment.

Parking at StarSpace 46 x
Parking is available 24/7 on the southside of Sheridan Ave in the large lot catty-corner across the street.

You are at risk of being ticketed or towed if you park directly in front of the SS46 building Monday thru Friday between 7:30AM and 4:00PM.

You are allowed to park in front of the SS46 building on weekends or after 4:00PM during the week.
